</div>
<div class="title">
OUR STORY
</div>
<p>
Zen Café was born from a simple idea: create a peaceful escape in the heart of the city. Two lifelong friends, both passionate about coffee and tranquility, decided to bring their vision to life. They dreamed of a place where people could relax, recharge, and savor every moment.
After months of planning, designing, and perfecting recipes, Zen Café opened its doors. Inspired by the calmness of nature and the art of mindfulness, they created a space filled with cozy corners, soothing décor, and a menu crafted with love.
Every cup of coffee and bite of pastry tells the story of their journey—a journey to create a little oasis of peace for everyone who walks through the doors. Welcome to Zen Café, where tranquility meets taste.
</p>
</section>
